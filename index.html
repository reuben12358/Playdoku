Playdoku

    How to Play

    Fill in the 3x3 grid with football players who match both the row and column categories.

    • Click a cell to search for a player
    • The player must have played for the club, be from the country, or match the category for both the row and column
    • Each cell can only be attempted once
    • A new puzzle appears every day!
